Make a Donation

We're an independent band. Every dollar goes directly toward new equipment, recording costs, and keeping the music going. We appreciate anything you can give.

Why donate?

We're a high school band working hard to grow. Every dollar you give goes directly toward what we need most:

  • New instruments and equipment
  • Travel Costs
  • Instrument Maintenance
  • Competition fees

No amount is too small — we appreciate every bit of support from our community.
